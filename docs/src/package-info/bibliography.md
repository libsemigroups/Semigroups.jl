# Bibliography

## Primary References

### libsemigroups

The underlying C++ library that powers Semigroups.jl:

- Mitchell, J. D. et al. (2024). *libsemigroups - C++ library for semigroups and monoids*.
  [https://github.com/libsemigroups/libsemigroups](https://github.com/libsemigroups/libsemigroups)

## Algorithms

### Froidure-Pin Algorithm

- Froidure, V., & Pin, J.-E. (1997). Algorithms for computing finite semigroups.
  In *Foundations of Computational Mathematics* (pp. 112-126). Springer.

### Knuth-Bendix Completion

- Knuth, D. E., & Bendix, P. B. (1970). Simple word problems in universal algebras.
  In *Computational Problems in Abstract Algebra* (pp. 263-297). Pergamon.

### Todd-Coxeter Algorithm

- Todd, J. A., & Coxeter, H. S. M. (1936). A practical method for enumerating cosets of a finite abstract group.
  *Proceedings of the Edinburgh Mathematical Society*, 5(1), 26-34.

### Schreier-Sims Algorithm

- Sims, C. C. (1970). Computational methods in the study of permutation groups.
  In *Computational Problems in Abstract Algebra* (pp. 169-183). Pergamon.
